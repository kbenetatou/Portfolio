ABOUT ME

Hello World, I’m Kassandra.

I’m into design, code, and figuring out how people actually use the digital stuff we build. I’m fascinated by the space where people and technology meet; especially when things don’t work quite right, something I’ve come to expect.

I’ve worked on a bit of everything — user research, interface design, prototyping — and I write a fair amount of code as well. My undergrad gave me solid experience with programming and databases, so I’m used to working across both design and development.

Professionally, I spent a couple of years at IBM in a project management office role: coordinating teams, supporting project progress, and managing details. It taught me a lot about how projects actually get done.

Oh, and this site? I built it myself (design, code, and all.) Hope you like it.
